What we do

Thing that keep us busy all the time

Tuesday

From 5:00 - 6:30 we have a weekly kids club, held at the Desmond Complex. Our night consists of Games, singing, memory work and a bible story. We do restrict this club to 5-11 years old

Wednesday

On the Third Wednesday of the month we have a Ladies Bible study. This also runs from 7:00 - 9:00 pm

Thursday

Our weekly prayer and bible study is held on Thursday night from 6:30 - 7:45 pm

Friday

EDGE youth group meets from 7pm - 9pm age 11+. Weekly youth group held at Desmond Complex. Runs from September to May

Saturday

On the second and fourth Saturday of the month we set up a Book Stall in the market square in Newcastle West. The purpose of this stand is to bring the gospel to people as they are out shopping in town, and also to advertise our meetings. We are in the square from 11:00-4:00pm

Sunday

10.00am Lord's Supper
11.15am Family Service and Sunday School
 6.00pm Bible Teaching...

First Sunday of the month we have a fellowship lunch.
All are invited. Each person should bring something to share for lunch.


KIDS Club

Program that runs for the little ones

Tuesdays

From 5:00 - 6:30 we have a weekly kids club, held at the Desmond Complex. Our night consists of Games, singing, memory work and a bible story. We do restrict this club to 5-11 years old

Easter Holiday Club

We will be holding our annual Easter Bible Club from Monday April 2nd to Friday April 6th.
Club will be held in the Desmond Complex, running from 10am to 1pm each day.
Cost is 5 euro per child for the week.

The club is restricted for 5 - 11 year old's only.

Included in the price is a Pandamania T-shirt.

EDGE

Experience and Discover God Everyday for youth group

Fridays

E.D.G.E is our Fellowship youth group. The letters stand for Experience and Discover God Everyday. We meet every two weeks at the Desmond Complex. We try to have one outside activity once a month whether that be bowling, skating, scavenger hunt etc. This group is open to teens from 13 -16 years. If you would like more information please feel free to contact us.
