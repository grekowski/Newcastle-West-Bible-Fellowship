Ultimate Decision

Gospel outreach throughout the Munster area

"The Ultimate Decision" is a gospel outreach which aims to have a gospel witness at various agricultural & county shows, market stalls and festivals throughout the Munster area.

We distribute freely Gospel literature, Bibles, CD's and DVD's and various other gospel material for both Adults and children. We are also selling small pictures and framed Bible Texts. Any finances made from the sale of these items is put back into the Lord's work to help cover the expenses of the work. If you would like to contact "The Ultimate Decision" or you have received some of our free material please do not hesitate to use the contact section on www.ncwbiblefellowship.com or contact Andrew & Gillian on 086 8744182 or Colin & Natalie on 085 7381970.

Reports

Once again, this year, we took the Ultimate Decision Tent to local agricultural shows between May and September. Despite generally wet weather which resulted in being towed out the field's at times we had an encouraging summer spreading the good news of the gospel. Our first was back in May at Drumcolliher and we were thankful to have the assistance of the TNT team from Canada. This resulted in a lot of literaure being given out and many interesting conversations with people.

Again we concentrated on local shows this year mainly because of the wet weather but visited Drumcolliher, Newcastle West, Charleville, Limerick and Cappamore shows. We are thankful for those who pray for this work and thank the Lord for the opportunities to pass out gospel literautre, Bibles and texts as well as gospel tracts as well.

This year we had a new screen at the front of the tent which through a digital projected showed a powerpoint presentation with verses of scripture and gospel messages. This is a great way to attract peoples attention as they walk past the tent.

Please pray with us that the seed of the gospel sown at the agricultural shows will be reaped in the salvation of souls.
